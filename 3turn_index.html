What is 3Turn Productions?

3 Turn Productions brings the joys of virtual worlds and online roleplay to the non-traditional gaming audience. By moving beyond the kill/loot model of MMORPGs and allowing the literature of the world to dictate the core game play, we offer a sandbox filled with toys which allow players to interact and build their stories while participating in game play activities designed to reinforce the world as characters would live in the author's works and time period.

Who we are...
